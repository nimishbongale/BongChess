Spectate

Top rated game from lichess.org.

Lichess provides Lichess TV, a place to spectate top rated games around the world and feel amazed at how well these rated players play chess.
Sit back and relax as you watch excitement reach the peak!

Puzzles

The daily puzzle from lichess.org.

Lichess provides Puzzles under the Tactic trainer, a place to really test your tactical abilities. The range of difficulty varies from puzzle to puzzle.
So go on then, have a shot at these!

Analyze

Analysis Tool from lichess.org.

Lichess provides Analysis board, a place to analyze your games against other players, or to play against a machine.
So no matter if you get thrashed or win a fantastic game, you never lose on learning something!

Board Editor

The simple board editor from lichess.org.

Lichess provides a board editor to play around with your pieces and visualize the whackiest of positions at your ease.
Try it out!
